Math just got a whole lot cooler.

Math Ninja is an iPhone/iPod touch game that makes practicing addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division fun!

You are Haruku, just an ordinary guy - except you're also a Math Ninja.

Use a combination of action, strategy, and quick wit to defend your treehouse against a hungry tomato and his robotic army!
